This is a Hubble Space Telescope image of Jupiter after pieces of comet Shoemaker-Levy 9 hit the planet. The dark spots are the impact sites. If it had hit the earth instead, you would not be reading this.

This illustrates quite clearly just how irrelevant we humans are in this universe. We humans like to think that we are each so significant, just so (self) important, but actually we are each an insignificant little nothing in a immense puzzle that, as a species, we have yet to grasp, let alone fully understand.

Let us humbly seek peace on earth, not just an absence of war, not just a fall in the gun death rates on our streets but true peace.
