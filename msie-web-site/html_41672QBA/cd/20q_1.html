Interview Questions

Assignment: You are a graphic designer who will be doing some designs for a new client. Before starting work on this assignment you want to know more about how the company perceives itself and how its customers perceive it.

 

 

 

20 Questions to Ask a Client:

These questions are hard to construct because each client will be different. I have constructed a large list, which I propose to use as a base for the next part of the homework.

I have limited experience of this and so I propose to make this up as I go. Hopefully, this list will help me to learn some lessons.

I would think that for each interview:

  • I would start off with a few top-level questions about the company and go from there.
  • I would have to chop and change my questions to get useful responses.
  • I would have to listen carefully.
  • My primary task would be to listen.
  • Many of the questions would not apply to specific cases.
  • Some questions would not be answered.
  • I will learn better questions, I will revise my lists.
  • I will improve my interview technique.

I would imagine that no two interviews would be the same.

The following is a lengthy list of possible questions. If you wish, click here to see a summary list of questions that I propose to use for a medium sized company.

Corporate:

  • What are your company vision, mission statement and strategic goals, and how does this project fit into them?
  • What is the company strategy for achieving those goals?
  • Are you a for-profit or a non-profit company?
  • What are the expectations of this project?
  • Does your company have a brand name and image?
  • What is the company culture?
  • What are your business partnerships and relationships? What do they provide? Is there a standard or a style that must be adhered to?
  • When and by whom was your company formed?
  • Where is your company's home office?
  • Does your company have an international presence? If yes, where and what kind of presence?
  • How many employees in the US? How many worldwide? How many by country?
  • What are your distribution channels?
  • Does your company already have a web site? If yes, what are its goals?
  • Does your company do business on the web? If yes, what business?
  • Does your company have an Intranet? If yes, what are its goals?

Products:

  • What is the current image of your products? Is this a key goal of the project?
  • What are your products and services? Are they the worlds best? If yes, how?
  • What is their positioning?
  • Do you have any patents?

Customers:

  • What is the demographic breakdown of your customer base?
  • Which group does this project target? How?
  • What do you provide to the various demographic groups?
  • Why do your customers choose your products and/or services?
  • What do your customers say about your company? How do you think your customer base perceives you as a company/organization?
  • What do your customers tell you need to be improved and/or implemented?

Competitors :

  • Who are your major competitors?
  • What do they do better, what do they do worse?
  • How are they represented on the web? What do you like about their representation?
  • What is their image?
  • How do your competitors perceive your company?

Design:

  • What is the impression you would like the designs to convey?
  • What message do you want to send to your audience?
  • What kind of visual appearance are you looking for?
  • What colors would you like in the site?
  • What should be the overall feel of the site?
  • How do you want your users to interact with the page?
  • What is the content of the site?
  • What is the overall design of the flow?
  • How/What is the navigation between the pages?
  • What is the balance of information and complexity vs. Ease of use?
  • If we were describing this project as a car, would you say it's a Chevy or a Cadillac? (German customers: a VW or a Mercedes?)
  • Is there an already existing look and feel and/or standards that must be followed?

Medium Sized Company Inc

  • What are your company vision, mission statement and strategic goals, and how does this project fit into them?
  • What are the expectations/goals of this web site?
  • Does your company have a brand name and image?
  • What is the company culture?
  • What are your products and services? Are they the worlds best? If yes, how?
  • What is their positioning?
  • What is the demographic breakdown of your customer base?
  • Which group(s) does this project target? How?
  • Who are your major competitors? What is their image? What do they do better, what do they do worse?
  • What are the content and functionality of the site?
  • What is the impression you would like the web site to convey?
  • How would you like to market position the web site? Up market? High Tech? Artistic? Youthful? Professional?
  • What kind of visual appearance are you looking for?
  • How do you want your users to interact with the site?
  • What is the balance of information and complexity vs. Ease of use?
