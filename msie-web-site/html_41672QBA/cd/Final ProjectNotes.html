Final Project Notes

 

 

Here are some thoughts when working on the project. If you find yourself stuck, or not sure where to start, just pick one of the things off this list:

  • Make a list of 20 things that you could do to improve the site. Next week add 10 more, then 10 more. The first 15 are usually easy. The next 15 are harder. The next 10 are the real gems. Ask your peer partner to help when you're stuck.
  • Go into detail with at least one of these ideas. You should have a description of how it works, wireframes, and possibly a simple flow chart.
  • Look at what databases will be used on this website. Also think a little bit about how these databases tie in with existing company databases.
  • Review competitor sites and see what best practices you should adopt
  • Outline who the users of this website are, and what the goals of the website are.
  • Pick a cool tool or technology that's on a website which is VERY different than yours. Change that tool or technology or implementation to fit within your website. For instance what if you had a "campus wide" Mapquest to find your way around the campus?
